lavender

Lavenders flourish best in dry, well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils in full sun. English lavender has a long germination process(14–28 days) and matures within 100–110 days. All types need little or no fertilizer and good air circulation. In areas of high humidity, root rot due to fungus infection can be a problem. Organic mulches can trap moisture around the plants' bases, encouraging root rot. Gravelly materials such as crushed rocks give better results .
